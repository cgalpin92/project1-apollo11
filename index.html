Who are we

Welcome to the Moon Landing Museum! On July 20th 1969 Neil Armstrong took man’s first steps on the Moon, it was an historic event which captivated the world. Here at the museum we showcase all things Apollo 11

What's Here

  • Over 20 one of a kind artifacts
  • Dozens of NASA and Russian Spaceflight Additions
  • The Actual Apollo 11 command module Columbia AND the Lunar Landing Pod, The Eagle

Our Website

Take a tour round our website where we’ll take you through the Timeline of the Mission and introduce you to the three men whose names have gone down in history; astronauts Neil Armstrong, Buzz Aldrin and Michael Collins. Our Visit us Page located at the bottom will give you all the information on how to find us. And for any Apollo 11 enthusiasts out there who are interested in volunteering with us, take a look at the Volunteering page where you can register your interest.
