Welcome

Welcome to the Moon Landing Museum! On July 20th 1969 Neil Armstrong took man’s first steps on the Moon, it was an historic event which captivated the world. Here at the museum we showcase all things Apollo 11. We have 20 one-of-a-kind artifacts, many of which were flown on this historic mission, plus dozens of NASA and Russian spaceflight additions. The highlight of the museum is the actual NASA Apollo 11 command module, Columbia.

So come and join us or take a tour round our website where we’ll take you through the Timeline of the Mission and introduce you to the three men whose names have gone down in history; astronauts Neil Armstrong, Buzz Aldrin and Michael Collins.

Our Visit us Page located at the bottom will give you all the information on how to find us. And for any Apollo 11 enthusiasts out there who are interested in volunteering with us, take a look at the Volunteering page where you can register your interest.
